weight: 3

First Baptist Church attendees may be included in a Grief Workshop series being offered at High Street Methodist Church on **Tuesday nights** from **6-8 PM** beginning **August 6** and continuing through **September 10**.  The group is being facilitated by Sandra Hardcastle, director of older adult programs at High Street.  The cost of the workshop is $18 to cover the cost of the book “Understanding Your Grief” by Alan Wolfelt.  There is room for a few more people in the class but they will be accepted on a First Come/First Served basis.  There will be sign up forms in the office beginning next Sunday and more details in the August *Visitor*.
